Fruit salad

Savor the colorful blend of fresh strawberries, blueberries, grapes, pineapple, bananas, and apple in this classic fruit salad. Drizzled with a hint of honey, it's a refreshing and vibrant dish that celebrates the natural sweetness and textures of seasonal fruits. Perfect for any occasion, this fruit salad is a delightful burst of flavors that brings freshness and joy to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup green grapes, halved
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks, fresh or canned
  • 2 bananas, sliced
  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • Juice of 1 lemon (about 2-3 tablespoons)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash all the fruits thoroughly under cold water. Prepare them as neededâ€”slice the strawberries, halve the grapes, chop the apple, and slice the bananas.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine all the prepared fruits: strawberries, blueberries, green grapes, pineapple chunks, bananas, and diced apple.
  3. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the fruit. The lemon juice not only adds a refreshing citrus flavor but also helps prevent the fruits from browning.
  4. If desired, drizzle a tablespoon of honey over the fruit for added sweetness. This step is optional and depends on your preference and the sweetness of the fruits.
  5. Gently toss the fruits together until they are evenly coated with lemon juice and honey, if using.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or transfer the fruit salad to a serving dish. Refrigerate the fruit salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the fruits to chill.
  7. Just before serving, give the fruit salad a final toss to redistribute any juices. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and extra freshness if desired.
  8. Serve the chilled fruit salad as a refreshing and healthy dessert or side dish. Enjoy the vibrant flavors and colorful assortment of seasonal fruits!

Feel free to customize this fruit salad by adding other favorite fruits such as kiwi, mango, oranges, or grapes according to availability and preference. Adjust the sweetness to your liking and enjoy this light and delicious treat!
